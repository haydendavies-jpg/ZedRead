02 — Typography
Serif for headings, sans for the interface.
Source Serif 4 (600/700) for page titles and card titles only. IBM Plex Sans (400–700) for every control, label, and body of text. IBM Plex Mono (400/500) for IDs, prices, and time ranges — anything tabular that benefits from fixed-width digits.

Numeric columnsRight-aligned or mono, font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums for prices/counts.
Category / tag chips.cbox — dashed-free solid 1px border, small colour dot for category identity, used inline in a cell rather than a full pill.
Do
Reuse .grow/.hcell/.gcell (or .mrow/.mcell for the wider status-driven layout) verbatim; only change the column count/widths per screen.
Don't
Invent a new row height, divider colour, or pill shape for a new table — extend this pattern instead of styling one-off.
